Risk Rating: High

X hit by ‘massive cyberattack’ amid Dark Storm’s DDoS claims

The Dark Storm hacktivist group claims responsibility for Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS) attacks causing global outages on X (formerly Twitter) on March 10, 2025. The platform had to activate Cloudflare's DDoS protections to mitigate the disruptions.

Key Insights:

  • Dark Storm's coordinated DDoS attack led to widespread service interruptions on X.
  • The platform resorted to Cloudflare's DDoS protection services to counter the assault.
  • The incident underscores the persistent threat posed by hacktivist groups targeting major social media platforms.

Analyst Notes:

This attack highlights the vulnerability of major communication platforms to DDoS attacks, which can disrupt services and affect millions of users globally. Organizations should implement robust DDoS mitigation strategies and collaborate with cybersecurity firms to enhance their defensive measures.

Risk Rating: Medium

US govt says Americans lost record $12.5 billion to fraud in 2024

The U.S. Federal Trade Commission (FTC) reported that Americans lost a record $12.5 billion to fraud in 2024, marking a 25% increase over the previous year.

Key Insights:

  • The financial losses due to fraud reached an all-time high in 2024.
  • The 25% increase from the previous year indicates a growing trend in fraudulent activities.
  • The data emphasizes the need for enhanced public awareness and preventive measures against fraud.

Analyst Notes:

The significant rise in fraud losses underscores the importance of continuous public education on recognizing and avoiding scams. Financial institutions and regulatory bodies must strengthen their fraud detection and prevention mechanisms to protect consumers.

Risk Rating: Medium

FTC will send $25.5 million to victims of tech support scams

The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) announced plans to distribute over $25.5 million in refunds to individuals misled by tech support companies Restoro and Reimage's scare tactics.

Key Insights:

  • Victims were deceived by false warnings prompting unnecessary tech support services.
  • The FTC's action aims to compensate those affected by these deceptive practices.
  • The case highlights the prevalence of tech support scams targeting unsuspecting users.

Analyst Notes:

Tech support scams continue to exploit users' lack of technical knowledge, leading to financial losses. Awareness campaigns and stringent enforcement actions are essential to deter such fraudulent activities and protect consumers.

Risk Rating: Low

Swiss critical sector faces new 24-hour cyberattack reporting rule

Switzerland's National Cybersecurity Centre (NCSC) introduced a new mandate requiring critical infrastructure organizations to report cyberattacks within 24 hours of discovery.

Key Insights:

  • The regulation aims to enhance the rapid response to cyber threats targeting critical sectors.
  • Timely reporting is expected to improve national cybersecurity resilience.
  • The initiative reflects a proactive approach to safeguarding essential services.

Analyst Notes:

Implementing prompt reporting requirements can significantly bolster a nation's ability to respond to cyber threats. Other countries may consider adopting similar measures to protect their critical infrastructure.

Risk Rating: Informational

Google paid $12 million in bug bounties last year to security researchers

Google awarded nearly $12 million in bug bounty rewards to 660 security researchers in 2024 through its Vulnerability Reward Program (VRP).

Key Insights:

  • The substantial payouts reflect Google's commitment to identifying and addressing security vulnerabilities.
  • The program incentivizes researchers to responsibly disclose security flaws.
  • Continuous investment in bug bounty programs enhances overall product security.

Analyst Notes:

Bug bounty programs are effective in harnessing external expertise to improve security. Organizations should consider implementing or expanding such initiatives to proactively identify and mitigate vulnerabilities.
